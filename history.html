OUR HISTORY

FROM 1950 to 2010

THE BEGINNING - 1950

Initially conceived as the Love Festival, the founder was then dumped by his girlfriend. Grown nihilistic with his hatred for romance, the founder changed the name to No Love Festival. In other words, the founder was a loser. He found new love soon after the first break up, however he forgot to change the name back again. He's dead now so we'll have to live with it I guess.

THE END - 1980

Closed down. Shut off. Signed out of OneDrive. Disconnected from Google. It's all over. It's the end. You know, it was good while it lasted. Been a great 30 years so far. Only a few cases of mismanagement and a few lawsuit on false advertising but it's the 1980s, who cares. People are too concerned over taking drugs to actually follow on anything.

THE REVIVAL - 2010

Basically Easter for No Love Festival. We rise again, from the ashes. Like a phoenix with its red flame of rebirth. The new festival is a little bit culty, but hey who cares when you can jam to music again on the Desktop stage! Hell yeah! Oh yeah to gain access to the festival a blood of a pheasant as well as your firstborn is required as payment. Dunno why the new owner is weird.
